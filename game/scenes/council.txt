You can see the leylines pulsing, the dimensional boundaries flexing, the collaborative magic that holds everything together flowing like blood through living veins.

The elders themselves are a study in contrasts: some ancient beyond measure, others surprisingly young but marked by the Spiral's favor. What they all share is a depth of understanding that goes beyond mere power. They've learned to listen. They've mastered the art of working together. And now they watch you, curious about what you might contribute to Avalon's ever-evolving story.
