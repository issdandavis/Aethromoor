The Council chamber exists in defiance of conventional spatial relationships. Suspended platforms orbit a central dais in patterns too precise to be natural, each one representing different magical traditions in carefully maintained balance. The architecture itself serves as ongoing demonstration of collaborative magic's fundamental principle: synthesis achieved through respectful dialogue between different approaches rather than dominance of any single methodology.

Between the platforms, holographic maps of the Spiral shimmer with information density that would overwhelm unprepared observers. Each map displays different aspects of the realm's multilayered geography—physical terrain overlaid with ley line networks, dimensional boundaries marked in colors representing stability gradients, temporal flow patterns showing where causality moves thematically rather than linearly. The maps update continuously, responding to real-time changes in magical infrastructure.

The elders occupy their platforms with bearing suggesting centuries of accumulated wisdom tempered by understanding that knowledge without compassion becomes tyranny. Each one wears insignia identifying their primary tradition—Dimensional Weaving, Chronothurgy, Boundary Maintenance, Collaborative Synthesis—but their presence here indicates commitment to integration rather than isolationist preservation of specialized expertise.

The chamber's atmosphere carries weight beyond mere air pressure. You can feel the accumulated decisions made here across generations, each choice shaping Avalon's development through course corrections that preserved core values while adapting to evolving circumstances. This is where theory meets governance, where abstract magical philosophy transforms into practical policies affecting thousands of lives.
