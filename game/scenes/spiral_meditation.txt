The meditation chamber floats suspended above the central Spiral. Translucent walls reveal the eternal dance of magical currents below. Cushions arranged in a circle invite stillness.

*if oathbound
  Your warden's training taught you discipline. That foundation may help here.
  *goto meditation_start
*else
  You've approached magic through study and wonder. Now you must find stillness within.
  *goto meditation_start

*label meditation_start
A master of the Third Thread—magic that learns and adapts—guides you. "The Spiral doesn't demand. It listens. What will you say?"

*choice
  #Meditate on duty and protection.
    You focus on oaths, shields, and the weight of responsibility. The Spiral responds with a steady, grounding rhythm.
    *set honor +10
    *if oathbound

      *set attunement +10
      *set council_trust +5
      *goto meditation_result
    The meditation is harder without an oath to anchor you.
    *set attunement +5
    *goto meditation_result
  #Open yourself completely to the Spiral's song.
    You release control, letting the currents of magic flow through you without resistance.
    *set attunement +15
    *if resonance_mark
      The mark flares. You and the Spiral harmonize perfectly, becoming one voice.
      *set attunement +10
      *goto meditation_result
    The experience is overwhelming but beautiful. You glimpse infinity.
    *goto meditation_result
  #Seek balance between duty and wonder.
    You hold both honor and curiosity in your heart, finding the point where they meet.
    *set honor +5
    *set attunement +10
    *set council_trust +10
    *goto meditation_result
  #Ask ${mentor} to meditate with you.

    Your combined focus creates unexpected resonance. The Third Thread master nods approvingly.
    *set attunement +10
    *set council_trust +15
    *goto meditation_result

The session ends. You rise from the cushion, changed.
