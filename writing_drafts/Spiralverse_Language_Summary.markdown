# Spiralverse Languages: A Summary

## Kor'aelin - The Binding Tongue
- **Origin**: Crafted post-Sundering to connect fractured realms.
- **Key Features**: 
  - Sacred particles (e.g., `'sil` for unity, `'thul` for spiral).
  - Vocabulary rich in dimensional, magical, and emotional terms.
- **Cultural Practice**: Dawn chanting to strengthen realm bonds.
- **Magic**: Binding spells and dimensional gates.

## Mal'kythric - The Severance Tongue
- **Origin**: Born in Shadowfell Depths to tear reality apart.
- **Key Features**: 
  - Cursed particles (e.g., `-kraz` for chaos, `-zhul` for termination).
  - Harsh alphabet and anti-resonance to Kor'aelin.
- **Cultural Practice**: Void screaming to rip reality.
- **Magic**: Sundering curses and void summons.

## Linguistic Conflict
- **Reality Fractures**: Simultaneous use causes dimensional chaos.
- **Syl'metric**: Neutral pidgin for safe communication.
