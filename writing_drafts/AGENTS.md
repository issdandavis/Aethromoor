## ğŸ¨ Scene Structure for Novel

**Effective scene template:**

1. **Opening Hook**: Sensory detail or character emotion
2. **Setting Grounding**: Where are we? What's the atmosphere?
3. **Character Introduction**: Who's present? What are they doing/feeling?
4. **Conflict/Goal**: What needs to happen in this scene?
5. **Rising Action**: Complications, dialogue, magical challenges
6. **Climax**: Peak of scene tension
7. **Resolution**: How does the scene end?
8. **Transition**: Bridge to next scene or chapter

**Example structure:**
```
Hook: "The boundary shimmered like oil on water, and Izack's 
       stomach twisted with anticipation."

Setting: "The practice chamber in Avalon's East Tower smelled of 
          chalk dust and ozone..."

Character: "Aria stood beside him, her precision-focused gaze 
            already analyzing the dimensional interface..."

[Continue with conflict, action, resolution, transition]
```
