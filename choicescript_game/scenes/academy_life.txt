*label morning_classes
*comment Morning classes depend on magical focus and chosen guild

"All students to their morning modules," Aria calls as the first bell of the day reverberates through the Spire.  

*choice
    #Attend Dimensional Theory with Izack
        *set energy -15
        *set academics +3
        *set izack_relationship +2
        Izack paces excitedly before a chalkboard filled with spirals, equations, and doodles of you tripping over your own feet.  
        "Dimensional resonance isn't just math," he says. "It's a song you have to feel. Now, everyone: hum along."
        *set collaboration +2
        *page_break
        In the middle of the lecture, he stops and frowns at a diagram. "Hmm. That shouldn't be there." He promptly erases the entire board.  
        "We'll revisit that when the chalk stops arguing with me," he says.  
        *return
    #Practice Boundary Weaving with Aria
        *set energy -15
        *set magic_control +3
        *set aria_relationship +2
        In a chamber lined with shifting stone rings, Aria demonstrates how to thread spells through boundaries without tearing them.  
        "Gentle pressure, like coaxing a cat out from under a couch," she explains.  
        You manage to weave a small portal that doesn't collapse. For a moment, you glimpse a sunlit beach on the other side before Aria closes it.  
        *set curiosity +2
        *return
    #Join Zara for Living Magic 101
        *set energy -15
        *set empathy +3
        *set zara_relationship +2
        Zara sits cross-legged in a grove of potted trees and explains how to listen to plant spirits.  
        "You can't force a tree to grow faster," she says. "You can only create conditions for growth and negotiate."  
        A vine climbs up your arm and curls affectionately around your wrist.  
        *set magic_control +2
        *return
    #Shadow Izack and Aria through a collaborative demonstration
        *set energy -20
        *set collaboration +4
        *set izack_relationship +1
        *set aria_relationship +1
        For one class, you watch Izack and Aria work together on a complex negotiation with reality: coaxing a stubborn staircase into aligning with the library.  
        The process is messy, brilliant, and involves a lot of muttered Runethic from Aria and enthusiastic Kor'aelin from Izack.  
        You learn more from their interplay than from any lecture.  
        *return
