The time-twisted garden is disorienting—beautiful but unsettling.

Plants don't just grow; they exist in all stages at once. You watch a rose that is simultaneously a tight bud, a fully-bloomed flower in peak beauty, and a withered seed pod heavy with next year's potential. The garden shimmers with temporal distortion, and colors seem to bleed across moments—yesterday's sunlight mixing with tomorrow's shadows in ways that make your eyes water.

*page_break

[b]"Don't think about it too hard,"[/b] Zara advises, her heterochromatic eyes showing sympathy for your temporal discomfort. [b]"Temporal magic gives everyone headaches at first."[/b]

*line_break

*comment Visual Note: Temporal Butterfly per canon - exists across past/present/future, wings display colors unnamed in linear time, leaves afterimages of motion

In the center of the garden, perched on a flower that's blooming and dying and blooming again, you see the Temporal Butterfly.

*page_break

Except "see" isn't quite adequate—you're witnessing something that challenges linear perception. The butterfly exists in three states overlaid: past (landing), present (resting), future (taking flight). Her wings are impossibly beautiful, displaying colors that don't exist in single-timeline reality—shades that are somehow both violet and haven't-been-named-yet, hues that are simultaneously fading and intensifying. Every movement leaves crystalline afterimages that persist for heartbeats before dissolving into temporal foam.

*page_break

[b]"She's tangled,"[/b] Zara explains softly, careful not to disturb the delicate temporal fabric. [b]"Sometimes Temporal Butterflies get their timelines knotted. She's stuck in a loop, reliving the same moment over and over."[/b]

*page_break

The butterfly seems aware of you across all three timeframes. One version turns its head to look at you while another is already watching and the third hasn't noticed yet. Her multichromatic wings shimmer with gentle desperation—conscious across past, present, and future of being trapped, but unable to escape the loop without help.

    The timelines snap into proper sequence.

    She flutters her wings experimentally, then spirals upward in joy.

    [b]"Beautifully done,"[/b] Zara says. [b]"That was advanced temporal work."[/b]

    *line_break

    *page_break

    Across all three timeframes, she focuses on you. Your stable, linear existence becomes a beacon.
