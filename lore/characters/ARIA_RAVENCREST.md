**The Archery Practice Encounter**

Izack first encounters Aria practicing archery on the Ravencrest Estate grounds after delivering captured bandits to Count Ravencrest.

**Key Moments:**
1. **The Frozen Moment**: Izack sees her and "time froze"

   - Recognition of complementary magical approaches

Their relationship develops through collaborative magical research:

- Political alliance benefits both families

**Wedding Ceremony:**
