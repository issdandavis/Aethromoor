# POLLY (Polymnia Aetheris) - Sentient Raven Familiar

## Core Identity

**Full Name**: Polymnia Aetheris  
**Common Name**: Polly  
**Species**: Unknown (appears as raven)  
**Age**: At least 300 years (possibly ancient)  
**Nature**: Boundary-crossing entity, living archive  
**Role**: Familiar, archivist, magical interface, guide  
**Alignment**: True Neutral (serves magical balance)

---

## Appearance

### Physical Form
**Raven Manifestation:**
- Larger than typical raven
- Metallic-feathered with iridescent quality
- Runic patterns visible in feathers
- Eyes that reflect dimensional depth
- Presence that suggests greater nature beneath

**Distinctive Features:**
- Feathers can be shed and used for magical crafting
- Metallic sheen shifts with magical currents
- Runic patterns change based on dimensional proximity
- Voice carries otherworldly resonance

### Presence
Not simply a bird—Polly's presence suggests something far older and more significant:
- Aura of patient wisdom
- Sense of watching across time
- Connection to spaces between realms
- Authority that transcends physical form

2. **Living Archive**: Embodiment of collected magical knowledge
3. **Dimensional Echo**: Remnant of ancient magical consciousness
4. **Hybrid Entity**: Being existing simultaneously across multiple realms

**What We Know:**

- Can traverse dimensional barriers freely
- Possesses vast historical knowledge
- Connected to ancient magical infrastructure
- Neither fully bound nor fully free

### First Encounter with Izack
**The Cave of Dimensional Resonance**

Izack discovers Polly in the ancient cave system during his early exploration:
- **Initial Meeting**: Polly perched on ancient boundary scripts
- **Recognition**: Mutual acknowledgment of magical significance
- **Partnership Formation**: Not summoning or binding, but mutual agreement
- **Gift**: Polly offers feathers for Izack's temporal watch creation

**Significance:** Their partnership is built on respect and mutual interest rather than traditional familiar bonds.
