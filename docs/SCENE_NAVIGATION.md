# Scene Navigation Guide - Polly's Wingscroll: The First Thread

## Overview
This document maps out all scenes and their connections to help with development and testing.

---

## Core Story Path (Linear Progression)

### Act 1: Arrival & Orientation
1. **startup.txt** - Character creation and introduction
2. **arrival.txt** - First arrival at Avalon, meet Polly
3. **familiar_selection.txt** (Optional) - Choose or bond with a magical familiar
4. **dorm_room.txt** - Customize your living space
5. **first_lesson.txt** - Learn collaborative magic basics

### Act 2: Academy Life & Exploration
6. **academy_life.txt** - Choose training focus (Aria/Izack/Zara)

**Optional Side Quests (can happen during academy life):**
- **library_secrets.txt** - Research and discover lore
- **creature_encounter.txt** - Bond with magical creatures
- **tournament.txt** - Participate in Harmonic Trials
- **midnight_mystery.txt** - Uncover Avalon's secrets at night
- **golem_workshop.txt** - Create a golem companion

7. **expedition_prep.txt** - Prepare for field expedition

### Act 3: Field Expeditions (Choose One)
8a. **singing_dunes.txt** - Truth-testing desert realm
8b. **verdant_tithe.txt** - Sentient forest realm
8c. **rune_glacier.txt** - Living ice realm

### Act 4: Deepening Bonds & Challenges
9. **character_bonds.txt** - Deepen relationships with characters
10. **romance_scenes.txt** (Optional) - Romance development
11. **secret_paths.txt** (Optional) - Discover hidden content

### Act 5: Climax & Resolution
12. **final_trial.txt** - Ultimate collaborative challenge
13. **endings.txt** - 14 different endings based on choices

## Scene Connections & Flow

### Library Secrets (Optional Scene)
**When**: During Academy Life phase
**Unlocks**: 
- Advanced knowledge paths
- Lore discoveries
- Relationship boosts with different masters

**Branches**:
1. Third Thread Research → Polly wisdom OR thread techniques
2. Boundary Research → Emergency protocols OR boundary art
3. Ecosystem Research → Symbiotic magic OR ecosystem healing
4. Hidden Stacks → Demon lore OR Fae Song OR Prophecy

**Affects**: 
- Knowledge stats (+10-40 various)
- Relationship with Polly (+15)
- Potential story callbacks in Final Trial

---

### Creature Encounter (Optional Scene)
**When**: During Academy Life phase
**Unlocks**:
- Magical companion bonds
- Special abilities/items
- Nature affinity increases

**Branches**:
1. Melodrake → Harmony singing OR magical shield
2. Temporal Butterfly → Timeline untangling OR empathy
3. Thoughtweaver Spider → Intellectual collaboration OR support
4. Phoenix → Hope for future OR understanding burden

**Affects**:
- Magical companion variable (melodrake/butterfly/spider/phoenix)
- Special items (Phoenix Feather, Thoughtweaver Web)
- Zara relationship (+10-20)

---

### Tournament (Optional Scene)
**When**: During Academy Life phase
**Unlocks**:
- Team dynamics testing
- Leadership opportunities
- Competitive endings

**Stages**:
1. **Harmony** - Maintain resonance through maze
2. **Crisis** - Solve collaborative emergency
3. **Synthesis** - All teams work together

**Team Options**:
- Hybrids (with Zara) - High synergy, natural fit
- Balanced - Theory + Practice + You
- Strangers - High risk, high reward
- Prodigies - Leadership challenge

**Affects**:
- Tournament result (champion/strong/participant/struggled)
- Leadership recognition flag
- Multiple relationship changes
- Collaboration +5 to +30

---

### Midnight Mystery (Optional Scene)
**When**: During Academy Life phase (can trigger at night)
**Unlocks**:
- Secret knowledge about Avalon
- Crisis awareness
- Responsibility roles

**Investigation Paths**:
1. **Spire** - Boundary strain crisis
   - Direct approach → Student Guardian Corps
   - Eavesdrop → Adaptive boundaries solution
   
2. **Vaults** - Zara's void negotiations
   - Observe → Secret knowledge
   - Intervene → Help Zara directly
   
3. **Gardens** - Reality Rose bloom
   - Stabilize → Technical achievement
   - Observe → Philosophical insight
   - Get help → Learning from Izack

**Affects**:
- Secret revealed flags (5 different secrets)
- Student Guardian Corps membership
- Innovative solution recognized
- Master relationships (+15-25)

## Circular Story Elements

### Library → Expeditions
Knowledge from library research provides context for expedition discoveries:
- Demon lore helps understand boundary threats
- Fae Song knowledge enhances musical interactions
- Ecosystem research pays off in Verdant Tithe

### Creatures → Final Trial
Bonded creatures may assist during Final Trial:
- Melodrake provides harmony support
- Temporal Butterfly helps with time-based challenges
- Thoughtweaver offers strategic insight
- Phoenix grants resurrection opportunity

### Tournament → Character Bonds
Tournament teammates become potential bond partners:
- Team dynamics carry over
- Vex/Thorn relationships continue
- Training partners from teams matter

### Midnight Mystery → Final Trial
Secrets discovered affect final challenge:
- Student Guardian Corps members have special roles
- Adaptive boundary knowledge unlocks unique solutions
- Void negotiation experience helps with dimensional threats

## Stat Requirements for Optimal Outcomes

### High Collaboration (80+)
- Unlocks: Collaborative Master ending
- Best outcomes in: Tournament, Final Trial
- Required for: Perfect Synthesis stage

### High Relationship (75+ with any master)
- Unlocks: Personal mentorship paths
- Best outcomes in: Character Bonds, Endings
- Grants: Special training opportunities

### High Knowledge Stats (60+)
- Unlocks: Advanced understanding in scenes
- Best outcomes in: Library, Expeditions
- Grants: Creative problem-solving options

### Balanced Stats (All 50+)
- Unlocks: Balanced Mage ending
- Best outcomes in: Multiple choice scenarios
- Grants: Adaptability
