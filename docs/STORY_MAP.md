## Quick Reference

### Critical Choice Points

1. **First Impression** (Arrival)
   - Sets initial relationship trajectory
   
2. **Training Focus** (Academy Life)
   - Determines which master mentors you
   
3. **Free Time Activities** (Free Time Hub)
   - Access to optional content (replayability)
   
4. **Expedition Choice** (Expedition Prep)
   - Major branching - only see 1 of 3 realms
   
5. **Bond Selection** (Character Bonds)
   - Deepens specific relationship
   
6. **Trial Approach** (Final Trial)
   - Determines ending type

### Stat Thresholds for Best Outcomes

- **Collaboration 80+**: Collaborative Master ending
- **Collaboration 60-79**: Scholar/Balanced endings
- **Collaboration 40-59**: Specialist endings
- **Collaboration <40**: Challenging endings

- **Any Relationship 75+**: Personal mentorship paths
- **Any Relationship 50-74**: Friendly interactions
- **Any Relationship <50**: Distant relationships

### Missable Content Checklist

- [ ] Free Time hub (easy to skip if rushing)
- [ ] Midnight Mystery (only triggers at night)
- [ ] Familiar Selection (early optional)
- [ ] Golem Workshop (side activity)
- [ ] Romance Scenes (requires enabled flag)
- [ ] Secret Paths (hidden, requires exploration)
